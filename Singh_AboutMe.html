About Me

Through this I was able to develop an interest for the growing field of computing and technology, and learn several programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Python, and C#. I was driven to learn these programming languages, because they provided me a lot of creative freedom, and allowed me to make a whatever I wanted. Learning C# allowed me to learn to make games, while HTML and CSS allowed for my growth in front end development In the future I want to pursue my interests in the field of computer science, and would want a career associated with that field. To get a better sense of the field and its emerging technology I constantly follow news, participate in competitions, and constantly involve myself in projects to keep improving my knowledge and experience in this fast-changing field.

The field of computer science is a very fast changing field, so it requires the enthusiasts to be very adaptive to the new technology. Through my daily activities, I believe that I am very suited for careers in this field because of my adaptiveness. Through the sports that I play, I have learnt to be very tolerant and energetic. The hard projects that I do teach me to be very diligent and innovative in my work, and always find new ways to do things. By my own expectations, I strive to be as helpful and versatile to other people as I can. Through the hard days of practice for sports, I have learnt to become a team player and very supportive of others. These qualities will help me in the future with my goal of pursuing a career in the field of computer science.

Through those hard classes, I was able to learn time-management and organization skills, and learned to complete all my work and have plenty of time to do other things. The support from my teammates kept me motivated, and allowed for my growth as a team player. I learned the valuable lesson of perseverance which taught me to be very strong and tolerant.
